About the Portal

The Kenya Online Voting Portal is a digital platform designed to allow Kenyan citizens to participate in elections through the internet.

This website was created by Mustafa Abdullahi to demonstrate how technology can improve transparency, accessibility, and efficiency in the voting process.He is a 1st year student at USIU-Africa.

Our mission is to make voting secure, simple, and accessible for all Kenyans regardless of their location.
